LOGIT 5.0 is our biggest update yet — a redesigned Summary and a whole new way to see your progress.

Progress tab
See your strength trend at a glance. Recent personal records and an overall progress score show exactly how far you've come, broken down by muscle group.

Weekly goal & streaks
The redesigned Weekly Goal screen tracks your streak week over week and keeps you chasing the next milestone. Your whole week sits right on the Summary, every day.

Muscle balance
A rebuilt Muscle Groups overview shows how your training is spread across your body and flags the groups falling behind, so nothing gets neglected.

Personal records, reimagined
Exercise details now lead with clean metric tiles, side-by-side comparisons and charts for weight, reps, volume and estimated 1RM.
